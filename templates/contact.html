Rodger van der Heijden

Creator of Triolingo

Triolingo was created by Rodger van der Heijden, a master's student of Data Science and Entrepreneurship at the Jheronimus Academy of Data Science (JADS). My passions include dealing with data, dashboarding and telling stories.

Though my mom is Polish, I unfortunately was not raised bilingual. Despite several attempts at learning the Polish language not being all that successful, I endured. With growing annoyances about the rigidity of existing tools I decided to create my own service; it would serve as a nice project to both increase my command of the Polish language and to try my hard at my first Flask project (which I learned completely on my own).

Even though this app is created merely for my own purposes, I recognize that others may benefit from its existence in some way, shape or form. Therefore I've open sourced the code in this GitHub repo. I'm open for pull or feature requests, or feedback in general.

Do you have any questions regarding the motivation? Of about the underlying choices? Or maybe you're just curious what the back-end looks like?

Or potentially you're just interested in who I am?

Shoot me a mail, find me on GitHub, or contact me on LinkedIn!
