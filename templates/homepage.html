Welcome to Triolingo

A tailored web app for language learning

Triolingo takes a very tailored approach in presenting you questions. For every sentence in the Polish language a score of your mastery is calculated. Using these masteries your understanding of sentences is calculated, with which the most appropriate for you can be selected. So, no more repetitive, trivial sentences about cats eating bread. Due to the large amount of sentences available even semi-advanced language learners can learn new words in context! And the best part is, the selected quiz difficulty changes along with you as your progress! So, moderately difficult questions will be moderately difficult for you, regardless whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner.

All data originates in part of a privately collected lexicon (consisting of ~500 manually selected words with translations); in part of the wonderful dataset of Tatoeba, released under a CC-BY 2.0 FR license; and (hopefully soon) in part of the massive NKJP dataset, which is released under a GNU license.

About

Some more details about the motivation

Triolingo is a side project of Rodger van der Heijden with a combined purpose of learning Flask and learning Polish. He had grown frustrated with the restrictions of comparable apps as Duolingo and Clozemaster, and grown curious to the possibilities of Flask. This website merely exists as a (functional) demo to showcase his efforts, but is not intended for actual educational purposes. In case of personal interest, you can clone the entire project from the Github repository, or you can contact Rodger via mail.

More information about the underlying motivation, the data preparation, and design decisions along the way can be found on the about page.
